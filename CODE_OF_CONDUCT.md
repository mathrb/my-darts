# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as its code of
conduct. The full text is the authoritative document and lives at:

- HTML: <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>
- Markdown source: <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/code_of_conduct.md>

By participating in this project — opening issues, submitting pull requests,
or otherwise interacting with the community — you agree to abide by its
terms.

## Reporting

To report concerns or violations, please contact the maintainer privately
through GitHub's private vulnerability reporting flow on this repository
(Security tab → Report a vulnerability). All reports are handled
confidentially.

## Enforcement

The maintainer will review reports in good faith and respond as soon as is
reasonably possible. Outcomes follow the **Enforcement Guidelines** section
of Contributor Covenant 2.1, escalating from a private warning through
temporary or permanent bans depending on the severity and pattern of
behaviour.
